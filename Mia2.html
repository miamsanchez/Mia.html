"But I can't run"
Success starts in the summer!
No one becomes a top varsity runner overnight. Everyone has to start somewhere, and it takes hard work and dedication. Summer mileage is KEY. It's important to come to summer workouts with the team to build up endurance so you can go fast in the fall.
